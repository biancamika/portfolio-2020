About Me 👁

I'm Bianca Aguilar, an Interdisciplinary Studies student (majoring in Information Design and Computer Science: Programming) at Ateneo de Manila University. As a visual designer and artist, I’m devoted to empowering social good in the digital sphere.

What I do

Specializing in brand and product, I’ve worked with personal brands and mission-based organizations in the fields of technology, innovation, career development, mental health, and feminism. Currently, I'm designing for a startup studio disrupting industries.

I'm also passionate about enabling youth creation, done through leading at Developh (as Chief Design Officer) and User Experience Society Ateneo (as AVP for Design Training).
